RWANDA

Rwanda is a land locked country located in central of East Africa with an area of 26,338 km2. It's population density per kilometer squared is 578/km2.

The map of RWANDA

More about RWANDA

  • Landlocked: Rwanda is a landlocked country meaning that it does not border any sea or ocean.
  • Ikinyarwanda: Ikinyarwanda is the mother language used by Rwandan.
  • Provinces: Rwanda is made up of four provinces (North,East,South and West province) and Kigali City (Capital city).
  • Population: Rwandans total population is 14.09 million after 2023 count.
